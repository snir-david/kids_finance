## Project Context

- **Project:** KidsFinance
- **Description:** Android app (Flutter + Firebase) for kids' financial literacy. Each child has three buckets — Money, Investments (multiplied by parents), and Charity (resets on donation). Multi-child, multi-parent.
- **Stack:** Flutter (Dart) + Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Cloud Functions)
- **Target Platform:** Android (primary), cross-platform expansion possible
- **Universe:** Iron Man (Marvel)
- **Created:** 2026-04-05
